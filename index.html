SSML Cheat Sheet

  • <break time="500ms"/> - Pause for 500 milliseconds
  • <emphasis level="strong">text</emphasis> - Emphasize text
  • <prosody rate="slow" pitch="high">text</prosody> - Adjust speed and pitch
  • <lang xml:lang="es-ES">text</lang> - Change language for specific text
  • <say-as interpret-as="date">2023-12-25</say-as> - Read as date
